Robbie's Review: Trina! (Trina Vega's One-Woman Show)

Trina! (Trina Vega's One-Woman Show)

Robbie here. Okay, so as you know I review stuff for TheSlap.com - movies, plays, books, lunch menus, etc... Unfortunately, EVERYTHING I have ever written on this site was deleted because Rex insulted TheSlap.com staff (multiple times). But he has since apologized, and "Robbie's Reviews" is back in business!

Today, I'm reviewing Trina Vega's one-woman show, simply entitled "Trina!"

And let me just say, that I am NOT writing a good review just to prevent Trina from smashing my face into my cream of mushroom soup... again.

No, really. Trina's show is fantastic. Expect a comedy and you won't be disappointed. You'll laugh your head off. My favorite part of the show was the hospital scene. When you think "hospital" you think there will be tears and sadness. But in this surprising twist, Trina actually evokes laughter from the audience. She's truly a gifted comedienne. And her neck brace looks very authentic.

I would write more about this incredible play but I don't want to give away all the funny. Come out and see it tonight. Tell Trina that Robbie sent you (and please remind her that violence against internet reviewers is never the answer).
