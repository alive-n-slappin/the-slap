Tap It Treats

 

It's hard to let a good deal go... and that is why we bought a bunch of stuff that we DID NOT need from Cat's favorite app -- Tap It.  In honor of our completely, wasteful spending, we're calling this week's trending topic: #TapItTreats
ToriVega
I bought 4 Deluxe Car Washes! I still don't have a car though.
 
TrinaVega
I got a 4-hour photo session for 30 bucks. I don't think 30 bucks have ever been spent so well.
 
RobbieShapiro
I bought 6 belly dancing classes. The deal said it would make me more attractive and daring. Here's to hoping...
 
AndreHarris
I got a 3-night stay at a 5-star resort in Greenland for only $79.99. Not sure when I'm gonna make it to Greenland, but when I do, I'm gonna do it in style.
