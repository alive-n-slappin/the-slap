A Very Sikowitz Christmas

A VERY SIKOWITZ CHRISTMAS

What up, Beck here. So, I've never done a blog before. I think I've never done it because I hate the word "blog." It just sounds so BLAHHHG. I totally felt that I had to BLOG about THIS though. 

I went to Sikowtiz's house to drop off my acting reel and I saw his Christmas decorations (Oh wait, excuse me, his ALL-INCLUSIVE WINTER CELEBRATION DECORATIONS -- as he calls them). I had to take a pic on my PearPhone to show you guys. 

 


If you'll notice, this is not a Christmas tree. It is a BUSH -- because Sikowitz doesn't believe in cutting down Christmas trees for entertainment. He says he's going to re-plant the bush in his greenhouse. You'll also see in this pic that Sikowitz used toilet paper to decorate his tree. "Why?" You ask. Because he is going to recycle the toilet paper after Christmas as well -- Don't ask how he plans on re-using it because he will SO tell you. And, one more thing to note, instead of buying a Christmas stocking, which he says promotes terrorism (We don't know why), he has used an old, dirty sock as his stocking this year. 

Merry ALL-INCLUSIVE WINTER CELEBRATION, Sikowitz. I'm glad we're not spending it together.
